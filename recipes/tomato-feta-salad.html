Ingredients:

  • 2 Plum Tomatoes
  • 1 Pound of Feta Cheese
  • 2 Green Bell Peppers
  • 1 Spanish Onion
  • 2 Lemons
  • EVOO

Steps:

  1. Cut up the tomatoes, onions,and peppers into pieces.
  2. Crumble up the feta cheese into smaller bite sized pieces.
  3. Next, take the lemon and squeeze out two tbsp of juice. Add to mixing bowl.
  4. Add the spices and EVOO to the mixing bowl. Mix all ingredients together.
  5. Mix the vegetables with the feta cheese in a large bowl. Pour over the lemon juice dressing and mix all contents up together.
